About Us

SINGH ENGINEERING PRIVATE LIMITED is a leading manufacturer and supplier of domestic as well as industrial use hardware products like High-Pressure Water Sand Blasting System, Jet Pump, Vehicle Washing System and many others. The innovative technology & experience enables us to supply quality products to our valued customers. Our facilities are range over Jamshedpur adjacent to Jharkhand.
